The Storming of the Bastille

What Happened?

  • Parisians were scared that troops were going to surround Paris and take down the revolution.
  • 800 Parisians assembled outside the Bastille in order to get weapons and gunpowder to defend themselves.
  • The Commander of the Bastille refused to open the gates and fired on the crowd.
  • Crowd broke through the defenses, killed the commander and 5 guards and released 7 prisoners.

The Bastille Falls

Aftermath

  • The victory proved to the monarchy that the citizens are capable of successfully revolting.
  • Caused many nobles to flee the France as émigrés.

Bastille Day

July 14, 1789

  • Bastille Day is celebrated as a national holiday in France.
  • Celebrated as France's "Independece Day."
  • During Bastille Day, people usually:
    • Parade around the city.
    • Have musical performances.
    • Watch fireworks.
    • Have picnics.
  • All businesses are closed, except for some bakeries and resturants.
